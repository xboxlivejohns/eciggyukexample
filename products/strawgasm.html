Flavour Breakdown

Strawgasm is built for dessert lovers. It's rich, layered and comforting — perfect as an after-dinner vape or a creamy all-day option.

Inhale

Fresh strawberry coulis with a touch of powdered sugar.

Exhale

Velvety whipped cream and vanilla custard.

Aftertaste

Soft biscuit crumble and lingering strawberry sweetness.

Best Enjoyed With

  • High-wattage sub-ohm tanks between 60–75W.
  • Rebuildable dripping atomisers for dense dessert clouds.
  • Nic salt boosters for extra smooth throat hit.
  • A cosy evening in — Strawgasm is your dessert course.

Mixing Tips

Add nic shots, shake for 60 seconds and let the bottle steep overnight to deepen the cream notes. For an instant treat, run a warm tap over the sealed bottle for a minute before shaking.
