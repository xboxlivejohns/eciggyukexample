Bubblegum Blitz 100ml Shortfill

Nostalgic pink bubblegum with a sprinkle of powdered sugar and a breezy cool finish — a playful all-day vape that never loses its flavour.

£14.99 Includes two optional 18mg nic shots for a silky 3mg blend.
  • Bottle size: 100ml e-liquid in a 120ml unicorn bottle
  • Nicotine ready: Space for two 10ml nic shots
  • Blend: 70% VG / 30% PG for dense yet smooth clouds

Flavour Breakdown

Bubblegum Blitz captures the rush of the first chew. Mouth-watering candy notes stay sweet without overpowering, making this an easy go-to for high wattage setups.

Inhale

Classic bubblegum syrup with hints of strawberry and banana candy.

Exhale

Cooling breeze keeps the sweetness in check and refreshes the palate.

Aftertaste

Light icing sugar finish reminiscent of your favourite corner-shop gum.

Compatibility & Setup

  • Ideal for sub-ohm tanks and pod mods with low-resistance coils.
  • Works brilliantly between 55–65W on mesh coil kits.
  • No added sweetener overload – keeps coils fresher for longer.
  • Childproof cap and tamper seal for safe transport.

Mixing Tips

Combine with two 18mg nicotine shots to reach a 3mg strength, or use salt nic for a smoother hit. Shake for at least 60 seconds and allow Bubblegum Blitz to breathe with the cap off for five minutes before your first fill.
