Mixing Tips

Each bottle lands with 50ml of zero-nicotine e-liquid and room for up to two 10ml nic shots. Add one 18mg shot to create 60ml at 3mg or two shots to make 70ml at 6mg. For a chilled cola slush effect, pair with a menthol nic shot, shake vigorously and leave to steep overnight for a perfectly blended soda vape.
