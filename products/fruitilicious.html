Flavour Breakdown

Fruiti-Licious Punch is built for fans of rainbow fruit blends. Each draw delivers waves of bright citrus, creamy mango and delicate berry to keep your palate guessing.

Inhale

Sweet pineapple and mango sorbet with a splash of dragon fruit.

Exhale

Refreshing kiwi and lychee accents with a breezy finish.

Aftertaste

Lingering berry sweetness with hints of creamy guava.

Perfect For

  • Vapers who love all-day tropical blends.
  • Mesh coil tanks running between 55–70W.
  • Mixing with menthol nic shots for a chilled punch bowl.
  • Pairing with summer BBQs and sunny afternoons.

Mixing Tips

Shake vigorously once nic shots are added and let the shortfill settle for ten minutes. For an extra juicy experience, leave the bottle open for a further five minutes to allow the flavours to bloom.
