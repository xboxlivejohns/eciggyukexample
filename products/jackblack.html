Jack Black 50ml Shortfill

Flavour Breakdown

Jack Black is the grown-up sweets experience with a frosty twist. Expect layers of spice and sweetness that evolve with every puff.

Inhale

Punchy aniseed and liquorice rope candy.

Exhale

Cooling menthol that brightens the herbal notes.

Aftertaste

Sweet sherbet finish with a lingering spice.

Vaper Notes

  • Pairs brilliantly with rebuildable tanks and mesh sub-ohm coils.
  • Best between 50–65W for peak flavour and smoothness.
  • Menthol lovers can add an extra ice nic shot without muting the sweets.
  • Compliant with UK regulations and manufactured in small batches.

Mixing Tips

Add one nic shot for 1.5mg or two for 3mg. Shake for 60 seconds and let Jack Black breathe for five minutes to soften the menthol edge before filling your tank.
