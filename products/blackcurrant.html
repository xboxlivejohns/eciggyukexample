How to Mix

Each bottle arrives with 50ml of zero-nicotine e-liquid and space for up to two 10ml nic shots. Add one 18mg shot to create 60ml at 3mg or two shots to reach 70ml at 6mg, then replace the top and shake vigorously for 30 seconds. Let the bottle settle for a few minutes to allow air bubbles to rise before vaping.

We recommend pairing Blackcurrant Burst with mesh sub-ohm coils between 50–70W for the fullest flavour.
